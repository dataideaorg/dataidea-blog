# Introducing Digital Certificates

![Digital Certificate](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1614036417651-efe5912149d8)

We're thrilled to announce that all our courses now come with VERIFIABLE CERTIFICATES! 
<!-- more -->

Upon completing any course, you'll receive:

 - Professional certificate with unique verification ID 
 - Direct verification link for LinkedIn 
 - Shareable proof of your achievement

For our alumni who've already completed courses - you can reach out for guidance on how to get yours.


## How It Works

### 1. Complete a Course

Focus on learning! When you finish a course and meet the completion requirements, our system automatically generates your certificate.

### 2. Receive Your Certificate

Your certificate will appear in:

- **Your Dashboard** - Under "My Certificates"
- **Certificates Page** - View all your achievements
- **Email Notification** - Instant notification when issued

## Adding to LinkedIn

!!! success "Professional Credential"
  Your DATAIDEA certificates are perfect for LinkedIn! Here's how to add them:

### Step-by-Step Guide

1. **Go to your LinkedIn profile** and click "Add profile section"
2. **Select "Licenses & Certifications"**
3. **Fill in the details**:
- **Name**: [Course Title] (e.g., "Python Fundamentals")
- **Issuing Organization**: DATAIDEA
- **Issue Date**: [Your certificate issue date]
- **Credential ID**: [Your Certificate Number] - *Click the copy button on your certificate!*
- **Credential URL**: [Your verification link]

4. **Click Save**

!!! tip "Quick Copy"
   On your certificate page, you'll find a handy **copy button** (üìã) next to your Certificate Number - just click it and paste directly into LinkedIn!
